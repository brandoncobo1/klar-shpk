Our Story

BUILDING ALBANIA'S FUTURE SINCE 2004

Founded in 2004, KLAR sh.p.k. has grown from a Tirana-based construction firm into one of Albania's most respected and trusted names in the built environment. Our journey spans two decades of delivering residential towers, commercial complexes, mixed-use developments, and hospitality projects that have permanently changed the skylines of Albanian cities.

Today we manage the full lifecycle of every project — from initial design consultation and permitting, through engineering and construction, all the way to handover and beyond. Our clients include private developers, institutional investors, and public bodies who trust us to deliver on time, on budget, and to the highest standard.

OUR VISION

"We strive to build more than just buildings — we create spaces that inspire life, foster growth, and embody the future of urban architecture. Every structure we raise is a statement about how we believe people deserve to live and work."

OUR MISSION

"We deliver integrated construction solutions that not only meet client expectations but also contribute to the sustainable urban and economic development of communities — because the buildings we put up today will shape the Albania of tomorrow."
